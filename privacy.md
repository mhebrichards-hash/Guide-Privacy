Guide Privacy Policy
Effective date: June 27, 2026

Who We Are
SF Guide is a Salesforce onboarding tool developed by Brandon Richards. For privacy questions, contact: mhe.brichards@gmail.com
What We Collect and Why

Google Account Information
  When you sign in, we use Google OAuth (via Chrome's built-in identity API) to authenticate you. We receive your Google account email address and display name solely to identify your account. We do not receive your Google password.
  Training Progress
  We store which step you have reached in a training guide. This data is tied to your trainer code and guide ID. It exists so your progress is preserved if you reload the page during a session.
  Trainer and Trainee Session State
  We store whether you are in trainer mode or trainee mode, and your linked trainer code. This is used to load the correct guide and display the correct interface.
  Guide Content
  Trainers create and publish training guides. Guide content (step instructions, element selectors, tooltip text) is stored in Google Cloud Firestore under the trainer's account.

What We Do Not Collect
  We do not collect Salesforce data, records, fields, or any content from your Salesforce org
  We do not collect browsing history or activity outside of Salesforce pages
  We do not use advertising or analytics trackers
  We do not sell data to any third party

Where Data Is Stored
  Session state and progress are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in local storage. Guide content and account information are stored in Google Cloud Firestore, hosted in the United States. All connections use HTTPS.
  How We Use Google APIs
  Use of information received from Google APIs complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Google account information is used only to authenticate you and is not shared with any third party.
  Data Retention and Deletion
  Local session data is cleared when you sign out. To request deletion of your Firestore account data, contact us at mhe.brichards@gmail.com and we will remove it within 30 days.
  Changes to This Policy
  If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance.
